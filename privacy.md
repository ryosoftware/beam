# Privacy Policy

Beam runs entirely on your device. It makes no network requests, has no
analytics or crash reporting, no ads, and no third-party SDKs. The app
has no `INTERNET` permission, so it cannot send data anywhere even if it
tried.

Your preferences (theme, notification, workaround settings) are saved
locally with Android `SharedPreferences`. Nothing is uploaded.

## Permissions

- `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` / `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE` — keep the battery
  notification alive
- `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` — show the status-bar notification (Android 13+)
- `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` — restart the notification after reboot
- `VIBRATE` — haptic feedback (optional, toggleable in Settings)

Nothing else.
